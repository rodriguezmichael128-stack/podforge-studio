Collection agreement

Creator authorizes PodForge to collect a 10% platform service fee for revenue generated by content created, edited, or published through PodForge.

For platform payouts paid directly to the creator, PodForge collects by saved payment method after each pay period statement is imported or synced, whether or not the platform payout has already reached the creator.

For PodForge-controlled payments, the fee is deducted at the transaction level before creator payout.

Production setup requires Stripe, clear authorization language, and lawyer-reviewed terms. Stripe setup has not been started.
